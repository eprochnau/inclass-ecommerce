The Tyrannosaurus is the most fierce of all dinosaurs!

The Tyrannosaurus lived during the [Late Cretaceous Period](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Cretaceous).

## Features

- Big teeth
- Little arms
- Thick tail
